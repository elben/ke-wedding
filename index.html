The Story

A story of rejection, hope and love.

Elben and Kristina were friends. Who introduced who to whom—even with all my prying of their closest friends—is lost history. But what matters is that they became acquaintances, and soon after, they became good friends. Best friends, in fact.

Not long after, Elben realized that he had fallen for her. He tried his best—as any gentleman would do—to run away from, hide and suppress his infatuation with this lovely friend. But at last he conceded to his emotions and asked her out on a date.

Kristina was—as any lady would expect—surprised. They went on the date, but Kristina found that her heart did not match his. "I just don't feel the same way," she said. Rejected, Elben flew away to San Francisco for the Summer and they both thought that marked the end.

But here the magic began. Kristina missed him and grew fond of him. And as confused as she was, she found that she wanted him.

Elben, of course, knew none of this. In fact, he considered staying in San Francisco for good. Oh how wretched it must have been for Kristina! But by Summer's end, Elben made up his mind: he would come back.

The day after his return, Kristina confessed her heart to him and they began dating that week.

On December 27th, 2012, Elben asked Kristina to be his wife, to which she replied: "Are you serious?! Yes!"
